About Us

Learn more about our mission and how our platform works.

Our Mission

At DrugOps, we are dedicated to leveraging technology for the betterment of society by addressing one of the most pressing issues of our time—illegal drug activities online.

Our mission is to combat this by providing a tool that uses state-of-the-art machine learning models to analyze and identify drug-related content, thus contributing to a safer online environment.

How Our Platform Works

User Submission

Users can submit links to public or group conversations from Telegram, Instagram, or WhatsApp that they suspect might contain illegal drug-related content.

Automated Data Collection

Our system accesses the conversation data associated with the submitted link and extracts all available text-based communication for analysis.

Advanced Machine Learning Analysis

Our AI model processes the collected data, detecting drug-related language and patterns. The model is continuously updated to adapt to new trends.

Suspicion Flagging and Reporting

Flagged conversations are compiled into a report for further review. If no suspicious activity is detected, the conversation is marked as clean.

Our Commitment to Privacy and Security

We understand the importance of privacy and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.

  • Data Confidentiality: All conversation data is treated with strict confidentiality.
  • Secure Processing: We use encryption methods to protect data during transmission and processing.
  • No Unnecessary Data Retention: Data is securely deleted after analysis.
