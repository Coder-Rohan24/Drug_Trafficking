About DrugOps

Welcome to DrugOps, a cutting-edge platform dedicated to keeping online communities safe from illegal drug activities. Our mission is to identify and report suspicious conversations that may involve drug-related content, ensuring a safer online environment for everyone.

Using advanced machine learning models, our platform scans the conversations in Telegram, Instagram, and WhatsApp channels. When users provide a link to a public or group conversation, our system thoroughly analyzes the content to detect any signs of drug-related discussions, sales, or advertisements.

At DrugOps we are committed to privacy and security.Read more...

Investigate a Channel

If you’ve come across a Telegram, Instagram, or WhatsApp channel that you suspect might be involved in illegal drug-related activities, we encourage you to report it. By reporting suspicious channels, you’re helping us make the internet a safer place for everyone.

Once reported, our advanced AI system will analyze the channel's conversations to detect any potential drug-related content. If the channel is found to be involved in illegal activities, appropriate action will be taken.
